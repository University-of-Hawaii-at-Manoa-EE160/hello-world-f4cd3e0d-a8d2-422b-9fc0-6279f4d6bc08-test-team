  • Workspaces

    Each student will give the TA their workspace url (e.g. https://c9.io/zhaol/ee160)

    • TA will ensure each student is using a private workspace
    • TA will ensure each student has bootstrapped their workspace correctly
      • wget -O - https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zhaol/ee160_scripts/master/bootstrap.rb | ruby
    • TA will record the URL and provide it to the instructor
    • When the TA and instructor request access to your private repository, please grant them access so they can view your code if and when you need help
    • It is up to each individual student if they want to give their group members or other classmates access to their private repositories; access can always be revoked later if special circumstances arise
